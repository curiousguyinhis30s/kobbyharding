# KOBBY HARDING - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**Official Name:** KOBBY HARDING
**Tagline:** "Authentic African Fashion, Modern Expression"
**Domain:** kobbyharding.com (future)

### Brand Story
KOBBY HARDING represents the fusion of traditional African craftsmanship with contemporary fashion design. Founded by Kobby Harding, the brand celebrates African heritage through modern, wearable art that tells stories of culture, identity, and innovation.

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary Logo:** KOBBY HARDING in custom typography
- **Secondary Logo:** KH monogram
- **Logo Placement:** Top-left navigation bar, footer center, product tags

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Deep Black:** #000000 - Primary background, luxury feel
- **Pure White:** #FFFFFF - Text, contrast elements
- **Gold Accent:** #D4AF37 - Premium highlights, CTAs

#### Secondary Colors
- **Warm Gray:** #8B8680 - Secondary text, subtle elements
- **Earth Brown:** #4A3C28 - African heritage connection
- **Deep Red:** #8B0000 - Sale tags, urgent CTAs

#### UI Colors
- **Success Green:** #10B981
- **Error Red:** #EF4444
- **Info Blue:** #3B82F6
- **Warning Amber:** #F59E0B

### Typography

#### Primary Fonts
- **Display/Headlines:** "Playfair Display" - Elegant, sophisticated
- **Body Text:** "Inter" - Clean, modern, readable
- **Accent Text:** "Bebas Neue" - Bold, contemporary

#### Font Hierarchy
- **H1:** 48px/56px - Playfair Display Bold
- **H2:** 36px/44px - Playfair Display Regular
- **H3:** 24px/32px - Inter Semibold
- **Body:** 16px/24px - Inter Regular
- **Small:** 14px/20px - Inter Regular
- **Caption:** 12px/16px - Inter Light

### Imagery Style

#### Photography Guidelines
- **Model Shots:** Professional, confident poses showcasing African pride
- **Product Shots:** Clean white/black backgrounds, multiple angles
- **Lifestyle Shots:** Urban settings mixing modern and traditional elements
- **Detail Shots:** Close-ups of fabric patterns, textures, craftsmanship

#### Image Requirements
- **Minimum Resolution:** 1920x1080 for hero images
- **Product Images:** 800x1200 minimum
- **Format:** WebP primary, JPEG fallback
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero: 16:9
  - Product Grid: 3:4
  - Product Detail: 1:1

### Icons & Graphics
- **Icon Style:** Outlined, minimalist (Lucide React icons)
- **Icon Size:** 20px standard, 24px for CTAs
- **Patterns:** Subtle African geometric patterns as backgrounds

## User Interface Guidelines

### Component Styling

#### Buttons
- **Primary:** Black background, white text, gold hover
- **Secondary:** White background, black text, gray hover
- **Accent:** Gold background, black text
- **Border Radius:** 8px
- **Padding:** 12px 24px

#### Cards
- **Background:** White with subtle shadow
- **Border:** 1px solid #E5E7EB
- **Border Radius:** 12px
- **Hover Effect:** Slight elevation, border color change to gold

#### Forms
- **Input Background:** White
- **Border:** 1px solid #D1D5DB
- **Focus State:** Gold border
- **Label:** Above input, Inter Medium 14px
- **Error State:** Red border with error message below

### Grid & Layout
- **Container Max Width:** 1280px
- **Grid Columns:** 12 column system
- **Gutter:** 24px desktop, 16px mobile
- **Breakpoints:**
  - Mobile: < 640px
  - Tablet: 640px - 1024px
  - Desktop: > 1024px

### Spacing System
- **Base Unit:** 8px
- **Scale:** 0, 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128
- **Component Spacing:** 24px between sections
- **Card Padding:** 16px mobile, 24px desktop

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Writing Style
- **Tone:** Confident, sophisticated, welcoming
- **Voice:** Professional yet approachable
- **Language:** Clear, concise, avoiding jargon

### Copy Guidelines
- **Headlines:** Bold statements celebrating African fashion
- **Product Descriptions:** Focus on craftsmanship, materials, cultural significance
- **CTAs:** Action-oriented, creating urgency without being pushy
- **Error Messages:** Helpful, friendly, solution-focused

### Example Copy
- **Homepage Hero:** "Where African Heritage Meets Modern Style"
- **Product CTA:** "Add to Your Collection"
- **Newsletter:** "Join the KOBBY HARDING Family"
- **404 Page:** "This page has wandered off. Let's get you back home."

## Digital Experience Principles

### Core Principles
1. **Authenticity:** Genuine representation of African fashion
2. **Accessibility:** Inclusive design for all users
3. **Performance:** Fast, responsive, seamless experience
4. **Storytelling:** Each piece tells a cultural story
5. **Quality:** Premium feel in every interaction

### User Journey
1. **Discovery:** Inspiring homepage showcasing collections
2. **Exploration:** Intuitive navigation, smart filters
3. **Consideration:** Detailed product views, size guides
4. **Purchase:** Streamlined checkout process
5. **Post-Purchase:** Order tracking, care instructions
